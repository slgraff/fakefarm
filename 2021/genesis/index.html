The Generations

Act I - The Greatest Short Story Told

In the beginning

Fairy tales begin with "Once upon a time"

YHVH Elohim

created the heavens

and

YHVH Elohim

created the earth.

(The End.)

Act II - Time Stood Still

& The earth had no form.

The earth was void.

Darkness was over the face of the deep.

Act III - A New Day Dawns

Day Zero

& Then the Spirit of YHVH Elohim visited

the place of desolation,

the face of the dark waters.

Day One

& YHVH Elohim summonded the light,

"Let there be light."

and light arrived.

YHVH Elohim saw the light.

YHVH Elohim said light is good.

YHVH Elohim

empowered the light

to overcome darkness.

YHVH Elohim

contrasted light and dark...

The good light YHVH Elohim named "Day"

The evil darkness YHVH Elohim named "Night"

The evil evening of dark void was done.

The good light arrived as morning.

That was good for One day.
